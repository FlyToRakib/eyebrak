DeepCycle CHANGELOG (formerly Eyebrak)
======================================

v2.0 - "The Ultimate Wellness Overhaul" (2026-03-12)
----------------------------------------------------
Major Release: The extension has been completely rebranded from Eyebrak to DeepCycle, bringing a brand new multi-sensory approach to digital wellness, gamification, and advanced productivity enforcement.

🚀 NEW CORE FEATURES:
- REBRAND: "Eyebrak" is now "DeepCycle" — reflecting a broader focus on deep work and holistic wellness.
- NEW: Virtual Plant Gamification — Your digital desk plant grows through 4 visual stages as you complete focus sessions, and decays if you stay idle too long!
- NEW: Advanced Health Breaks system with customizable physical break routines (e.g., eye rests, stretching).
- NEW: Multi-sensory 3-way alerts (Overlay, Notification, and looping Continuous Audio Beep).
- NEW: Custom Sound Library — Upload your own MP3/WAV files for notifications and ambient noise.
- NEW: Muslim Salat Reminder — Automatically fetches and schedules desktop notifications for the 5 daily prayers based on your city/country.
- NEW: Custom Reminders Engine — Create one-time or repeating custom interval text alerts.
- NEW: Focus Mode (Site Blocker) to strictly block distracting websites (facebook.com, youtube.com, etc.) during Pomodoro "Work" phases via a beautiful dedicated block page.
- NEW: Hydration Tracker to track your daily water intake directly from the popup.
- NEW: Smart Idle Detection to automatically pause the timer when you're away from your PC.
- NEW: Activity Heatmap in Stats showing your active minutes per hour.
- NEW: Streak system with daily reset ('🔥 Level Up!' streak toast notification after each completed Pomodoro cycle).

✨ UI & IMPROVEMENTS:
- IMPROVED: Fully animated SVG countdown circle overlays for Pomodoro Focus Breaks, matching the beautiful aesthetic of Health Breaks.
- IMPROVED: Overlays now feature a 70% opacity dark glassmorphism background to heavily dim distracting active tabs without entirely hiding them.
- IMPROVED: Options page has been completely redesigned with a card-based layout, organized settings, and a new strict mode lock.
- IMPROVED: Smarter Idle Detection prevents Pomodoro timer desyncs when stepping away from the keyboard.
- IMPROVED: Real-time dynamic alarm synchronization ensures settings changes trigger immediate background updates without reloading.
- IMPROVED: Timer ring uses a blue-to-cyan gradient stroke with glow effect and pulses when paused or in final 60 seconds.
- IMPROVED: Body theme shifts to green accent during Pomodoro Break phases.

🐛 BUG FIXES:
- FIX: Resolved issue where the Continuous Beep failed to trigger due to a missing variable registration.
- FIX: Resolved issue where the Health Break beep card was permanently hidden in the settings UI.
- FIX: Resolved Chrome extension alarm limitations by enforcing valid minimum interval schedules for fast-looping beeps.
- FIX: Fixed bug where default Health Break routines were not saving on initial vanilla installations.
- FIX: Fixed issue where Pomodoro breaks were only displaying a rudimentary breathing animation instead of a proper countdown clock.
- FIX: Settings page Save button now works reliably with null-safe JS.
- FIX: Race condition between updateAlarm and updateSettings removed.
